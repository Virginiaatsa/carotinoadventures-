Backpacker Tips

Stuff I wish someone had told me before my first big trip.

01

Book flights 6–8 weeks out

Sweet spot for budget flights. Too early and prices are high; too late and they spike again.

02

Eat where locals eat

A meal steps away from a tourist street can cost 5x less and taste 10x better.

03

Overnight buses = free accommodation

You travel + sleep at the same time. Use it on long legs between cities.

04

Pakistan is safer than the news says

Tourist regions like Hunza and Gilgit-Baltistan are incredibly welcoming. Do your research.

05

Get travel insurance — always

The one thing you shouldn't cut corners on. It's cheap. Medical bills abroad aren't.

06

Learn 10 words in the local language

Hello, thank you, how much, delicious. It opens more doors than any guide book.
